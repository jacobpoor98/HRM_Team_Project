About

Get to know us.

  • 2019

    Our Inspiration

    In 2019 we took our family on a vacation to Mexico. I wanted to do some fun cultural experiences with the kids. I found a chocolate making experience in Merida at a place called "Fela Chocolate". We signed up and had an amazing time. (Probably my favorite thing we did/saw on the trip) At this experience we learned the history of chocolate and how it was made. I was just so enthralled!

  • Early 2021

    Getting Serious

    In 2021 I started seriously researching chocolate. I watched YouTube videos, read websites and blogs, anything I could find to learn more about chocolate. On this journey I discovered things I had no idea about (origin chocolate, bean-to-bar, what fair trade was, etc).

  • Late 2021

    Humble Beginnings

    In a leap of faith I purchased the supplies that would take my casual interest into a full blown hobby! At the beginning there were so many flops, delicious flops, but flops nonetheless. Eventually all the pieces came together into a perfect chocolate bar! I started sharing my chocolate with others and they loved it too.

  • December 2022

    The Future

    As I have been sharing my knowledge and delicious chocolates, of course, I have been encouraged sometimes aggressively, to turn this hobby into a small business. I love the process of making chocolate. It is so scientific and simple yet complex. And I have discovered that I really enjoy teaching others all that I have learned about chocolate.

Impact on the Farmers & Farm Environments

100% of all beans used for Impact Chocolate are responsibly sourced. No child labor is used in bean farming or production, farmers are paid a living wage and farms utilize sustainable transparent practices.
